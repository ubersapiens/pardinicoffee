Farm.

The origin. That's where all the answers are. The altitude, the rainfall, the temperature and human ability all bind together to make the ideal coffee cherry. But it doesn't end there. To understand coffee and its intricate ecosystem you must go down to the basics: the farm.

We visit and work in every farm we intend to source before purchasing their coffee. We look at processing, drying, agro conditions, cherry picking, lot grouping and of course, talent.

All of our coffee is traceable down to the farmer that picked the fruit. We trace craftsmanship and talent back to origin.
