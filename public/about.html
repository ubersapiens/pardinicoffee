About.

Pardini Coffee Company is a coffee think tank based in Paris concentrated in research and craft. We focus on the scientific and aesthetic elements necessary to make the perfect coffee.

We are also a coffee roasting company devoted to harnessing the rarest, finest grains.

Our founder, Juan Francisco Pardini, was born and raised in Panama, tasted his first v60 in NYC, learned to roast in Stockholm and pulled his first espresso in Paris.
