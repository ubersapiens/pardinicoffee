About.

Pardini Coffee Experience is a coffee think tank based in Paris concentrated in research and craft. We focus our analyses on the scientific and aesthetic elements necessary to make the perfect coffee.

We are also a coffee roasting company devoted to harnessing the rarest, finest grains.

Our founder, Juan Francisco Pardini, was born in Panama, tasted his first v60 in NYC, learned to roast in Stockholm and pulled his first 28s espresso in Paris.
