Coffee.

There is a vast world beyond that sweet taste we crave from the minute we open our eyes. From the way it is dry-processed to the time and rate at which it needs to be filtered to the size of its grounds. Coffee is no longer a commodity. It is a delicacy.

We like to think that we are as close to origin as you can get. To enhance that experience, we only source beans that have a connection to us. We pick, process, roast and filter in a way that taps each one of those selected profiles. For now, Panama is our source.

In the coffee lab, we are not always roasting, we like to experiment with dosage, grinding, water temperature and agitation to manipulate the final product and to further develop our understanding of the bean.
